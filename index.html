Retail Sales Analysis

Retail Sales Analysis

This project showcases a comprehensive Retail Sales Dashboard created from scratch using Excel. The dashboard is designed to provide an interactive and visual summary of key business metrics for a retail store. The goal of the dashboard is to help stakeholders understand sales performance, profitability, and trends in a clear and engaging manner.

Project Details

KPIs are displayed at the top of the dashboard for quick insights into overall business performance.

Profit by Sub-Category:A Treemap chart visualizes profitability across different product sub-categories. For example, Printers and Bookcases are top-performing categories, while some categories have lower contributions.

Monthly Profit Analysis:A column chart highlights profit trends across the months. The red bars indicate months with negative profit, providing a clear view of underperforming periods.

Sales by Category:A pie chart shows the distribution of sales quantity across Clothing, Electronics, and Furniture categories, making it easy to identify which category contributes the most.

Top 5 States by Sales:A bar chart ranks the top-performing states based on total sales. Maharashtra leads, followed by Madhya Pradesh and others.

Quantity Ordered by Payment Modes:A donut chart provides insights into customer preferences for payment methods, such as COD, UPI, Credit Card, and more. COD emerges as the most popular payment method.

Quarterly Sales Filters:Slicers allow users to filter sales data by quarters, offering flexibility for analyzing trends in specific timeframes.

Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

This project involves the creation of an interactive and visually appealing dashboard to analyze and monitor the sales performance of a coffee shop. The dashboard consolidates key metrics, charts, and visualizations to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

Project Details

The goal of this project was to understand customer preferences, identify top-performing products, track sales trends, and optimize business operations through data visualization.

As a fresher and an aspiring data analyst, this project allowed me to: Showcase my ability to transform raw data into meaningful insights. Demonstrate my proficiency in Microsoft Excel as a tool for storytelling through data.

Why This Project Stands Out: This dashboard is a testament to my passion for working with data. It combines analytical thinking with creative visualization to deliver actionable insights. The interactive nature of the dashboard ensures that users can explore data dynamically to answer business-critical questions.

What I Learned: How to represent complex data in a simple and easy-to-understand manner. The importance of focusing on actionable insights that add value to decision-making. Advanced Excel features like slicers, conditional formatting, and chart optimization.

Reliance Sales Analysis

Reliance Sales Analysis

This project demonstrates my ability to analyze and visualize sales data effectively using Power BI. The report provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, including key metrics such as total sales, profit, and units sold. It features interactive dashboards that allow users to explore trends across regions, product categories, and time periods.

Highlights of the project include:

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): Visual representation of total revenue, profit margins, and sales growth.

Regional Insights: Analysis of sales distribution and performance across different regions to identify high-performing and underperforming areas.

Product Category Performance: Insights into the best-selling products and categories, helping to identify opportunities for growth.

Trend Analysis: Time-based analysis to understand seasonal patterns and monthly trends in sales data.

Interactive Filters: Dynamic slicers and filters for users to drill down into specific data segments.

This project showcases my skills in data modeling, DAX, and crafting visually appealing and insightful dashboards tailored for decision-making.
